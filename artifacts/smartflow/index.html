About Us

DQ Developments Ltd provides professional building and property development services for residential projects. Led by Anthony Digle, the company focuses on dependable service, attention to detail and quality workmanship from the early stages of a project through to completion.

Our Services

  • Home Extensions — rear, side and double-storey extensions to add space and value
  • Property Renovations — full and partial renovations for existing residential properties
  • Brickwork & Blockwork — quality brickwork for new builds and extensions
  • Roofing — pitched and flat roof construction including tiling and Velux installations
  • Structural Alterations — load-bearing wall removals and structural modifications
  • Bi-Fold Door Installation — aluminium bi-fold and patio door fitting
  • General Building Work — all aspects of residential construction and maintenance
  • Property Development — residential property acquisition, conversion and development
